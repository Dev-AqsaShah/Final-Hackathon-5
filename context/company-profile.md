# TechNova SaaS - Company Profile

## Company Overview
**Name:** TechNova Inc.
**Product:** TechNova Platform - A project management and team collaboration SaaS
**Founded:** 2019
**Size:** 150 employees, 8,000+ active customers
**Headquarters:** San Francisco, CA

## Product Description
TechNova Platform helps teams manage projects, track tasks, collaborate in real-time,
and generate productivity reports. It integrates with Slack, GitHub, Jira, and Google Workspace.

## Pricing Plans
- **Starter:** $12/user/month (up to 10 users)
- **Professional:** $24/user/month (unlimited users, advanced features)
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing (SSO, SLA, dedicated support)

**Note:** Pricing negotiations and custom quotes must be escalated to Sales team.

## Key Features
1. **Task Management** - Create, assign, track tasks with deadlines
2. **Project Boards** - Kanban, Scrum, and List views
3. **Time Tracking** - Built-in timer and manual logging
4. **Reports & Analytics** - Productivity, burndown, velocity charts
5. **Integrations** - 50+ third-party integrations
6. **API Access** - REST API with webhook support (Professional+)
7. **File Storage** - 10GB (Starter), 100GB (Professional), Unlimited (Enterprise)
8. **Team Chat** - Built-in messaging with channels

## Support Team Structure
- **AI Agent (Tier 1):** Handles routine queries 24/7
- **Human Support (Tier 2):** Complex issues, Mon-Fri 9am-6pm PST
- **Account Managers (Tier 3):** Enterprise clients, billing disputes
